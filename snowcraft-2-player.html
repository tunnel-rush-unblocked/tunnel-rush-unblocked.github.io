Snowcraft - 2 Player

Join Steve and Alex on a new adventure in the world of Snowcraft with snowy landscapes and rapidly dropping temperatures. Are you brave enough to overcome it?

In Snowcraft - 2 Player, your mission is to help the two characters move to the portal before it freezes. Everything is gradually freezing, helping the duo reach another dimension by moving them as fast as possible. However, on the way, you must help them overcome a series of deadly sharp traps. Besides, the evil snow monsters are raging and blocking your way. Use Steve's sword to defeat them and continue the journey.

Besides, you can also use Alex to collect diamonds in each level in Snowcraft - 2 Player. These diamonds will help you activate the Nether portal, and collect enough diamonds to continue. The portal is at the end of each level, move them safely there to continue. If either character collides with an obstacle or an enemy, the game will end immediately. Explore all the challenging levels in this game now!

How to play

  • Use WASD to control Alex
  • Press arrow keys to control Steve.
